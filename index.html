Founded With Purpose

Dreux Property Solutions was founded by a woman entrepreneur with a deep commitment to stability, accountability, and long-term community impact.

Built from lived experience and a desire to create meaningful change, the company focuses on restoring and maintaining homes that provide families with safe, dependable places to live.

This work is rooted in responsibility, consistency, and respect for the communities being served.
